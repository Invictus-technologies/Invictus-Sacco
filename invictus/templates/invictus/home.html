what makes
Invictus Sacco
Unique?

Invictus Sacco is a Savings & Credit Society registered in Kenya since 6th July 1973 and has been licensed by SASRA from 22nd July 2013 to operate Front Office services. We promote thrift among our members by mobilizing savings and create a source (pool) of funds for onward lending at a fair and reasonable rate of interest. We offer various Back-office loan products namely Normal, Emergency, School Fees and Jiinue Loans. Our Front office products include advances such as Salary, Bridging, Karibu, Premium Financing, One, Three, Six and 12 Months Advances. Members can further save by opening School Fee, Holiday, and Fixed Deposit Accounts.

We have a wide reach of members all over the world who have come to depend on us as a trusted, dependable, stable and growing Sacco. We have a wide range of products to suit every member – from a child with a bright future to the oldest grandmother enjoying a great life.

About us
Invictus Sacco is a Savings and Credit Co-operative Society registered in 1973 under the Co-operative Societies Act of the Laws of Kenya. The Sacco primarily mobilizes Savings and Deposits and extends Credit to its members thereby empowering them economically and socially.
